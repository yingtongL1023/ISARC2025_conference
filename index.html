Abstract
To address the challenges in 3D rebar detection, we propose a synthetic dataset generation pipeline that simulates real-world rebar scenes with photorealistic rendering and domain randomization. This enables large-scale training of deep models without labor-intensive manual annotations. We evaluate our approach on multiple test sets and demonstrate its effectiveness in improving segmentation accuracy on real data.
